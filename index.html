about rebecca

Rebecca Wong is an amateur baker and cake decorator that makes all kinds of sweet treats: from tarts to eclairs to cakes.

Salted Caramel Mini Apple Pie Roses

These apple pie roses, inspired by a combination of pies and tarts, allow each person to have a personal rose for dessert rather than a pie to share.

With a flaky pie crust in the shape of a flower, crisp baked apples shaped into a rose, and a smooth salted caramel filling, they combine the best of both worlds from pies and tarts.

Spring Floral Cake

It's an edible bouquet which happens to be absolutely delicious.

This decadent s'mores layer cake is topped with hand piped buttercream flowers and leaves. For a fresh springy feel, some lavendar oil can be added to the buttercream to make it look and smell just like a garden.

Opera Cake

The cake that'll make your taste buds sing.

The French opera cake is centered around the flavors of coffee and chocolate. These dainty little cakes are topped with chocolate decoration.

succulent cake

Dessert or desert?

This s'mores cake has been decorated with hand-piped buttercream succulents and a graham cracker crumb sand desert.


wreath cake

This rustic naked layer cake is inspired by the decorative winter wreaths.

Adorned with buttercream pine cones, hand piped leaves, and a sprinkling of coarse sugar snow, this cake is ready for the winter.

Peppermint Matcha Macarons

These peppermint matcha macarons are sophisticated and festive at the same time. The macaron shell has a subtle matcha flavoring and it's filled with a decadent hazelnut filling. The edges are garnished with crushed candy canes for a holiday twist and additional crunch.

Salted Caramel Chocolate Cake

This chocolate cake with a salted caramel filling and a whipped chocolate buttercream is topped with an buttercream roses, blueberries, and strawberries.
